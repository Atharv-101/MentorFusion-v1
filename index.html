FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of payments can I process through GeekPay?

GeekPay supports both ACH and credit card payments, offering a broad spectrum of payment options for your borrowers. You can process one-time payments and set up recurring payments with ease.

How does GeekPay simplify my loan management?

GeekPay is designed to streamline your loan management process. It provides automated loan tracking and ledgering, reducing the manual work involved in keeping tabs on payments and loan statuses. GeekPay also offers a reporting feature that gives you portfolio-level visibility into loan performance and status.

How does GeekPay enhance transparency and communication with borrowers?

GeekPay includes a self-service borrower portal, which allows borrowers to access their loan information at any time. This fosters complete transparency in loan management. Automated email notifications are also part of the package, ensuring that both lenders and borrowers stay informed about payment due dates, payment clearance, and other essential updates.

How does GeekPay ensure the safety and compliance of my loan management process?

GeekPay values your data security. Our platform maintains full compliance with CPAA, BSA/AML, NACHA, and PCI rules and regulations. Also, with long-standing relationships with banks and payment processors, GeekPay offers a secure, compliant, commercial-grade solution that you can trust for managing your loans.
